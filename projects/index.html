Projects

I build automation and integration tooling around telecom deliverables, HR systems, finance workflows, and Quickbase apps — mostly using Python, Google Cloud Functions, Quickbase, and Excel/Box integrations.

This page groups my work into themes and shows how individual cloud functions, pipelines, and templates fit into bigger systems.

HR & Timekeeping Automations (Kronos + Quickbase)

A suite of automations around Kronos and Quickbase to keep HR data accurate without manual data entry.

  • Kronos Weekly Timesheets Sync (kronos_timesheets) – Google Cloud Function that updates weekly timesheet records in Quickbase from Kronos data.
  • Kronos Holiday Calendar Automation (kronos_holiday) – Automates holiday-related logic so HR rules are consistently applied.
  • Employee Out-of-Office to HR Sync (kronos-outofoffice-email) – Reads employee out-of-office / off work info and pushes a weekly summary to HR/Quickbase.
  • Kronos Connector Pipeline (kronos-connector-pipeline) – Integration pipeline that glues together Kronos, Quickbase, and cloud functions so data flows automatically.

Finance & Procurement Automation (Ariba + Bulk Uploads)

Tools that help finance and project teams work with Ariba data and large sets of PO lines without copy/paste.

Finance Bulk Upload & Ariba Integration Toolkit

A group of scripts and bulk-upload tools that move structured PO data into our finance system:

  • verizon_ariba_import – GCP data import for Verizon Ariba feeds
  • verizon_ariba_core_sync – keeps internal “core” data in sync with Ariba
  • PO_Line_Codes – bulk upload for finance
  • POLineItems – bulk upload for finance
  • pricing_table – bulk upload for finance
  • PurchaseOrder&ChangeOrderRequest – bulk upload for finance
  • Quotes_and_POR – bulk upload for finance
  • Vendor_PO_Line_Items – bulk upload for finance

Together, these replace manual spreadsheet massaging and let finance ingest PO and pricing data in a repeatable way.

New Award & PO Line Notifications from Ariba – Notifications when new project awards or new PO lines arrive from Ariba, so project managers and finance don’t miss changes buried in the portal.

Document & Compliance Automation (Telecom)

These projects turn carrier deliverables and compliance documents into automated workflows.

  • T-Mobile Close-Out Memo Automation (tmobile_close_out_memo) – Takes project data from Quickbase, fills a standardized T-Mobile Close-Out Memo template, and uploads the finished memo to the correct Box folder.
  • Verizon SCIP Template Automation (vz-scip-template) – Pulls data from Quickbase, cleans and transforms it with pandas, and maps it into a structured Verizon SCIP workbook.
  • T-Mobile HMBP Notification & Documents (tmo-hmbp-notification) – Automation for generating and managing HMBP-related documents/forms and notifying the right people.
  • T-Mobile CPUC/CLON File Automation (tmobile_cpuclon_file) – Generates CPUC/CLON or similar regulatory files automatically from project/site data.
  • Core Telecom Noise Study Templates (core-noise-study) – Reusable noise study templates for jurisdictions like Bakersfield, Santa Rosa, and Santa Cruz. See: Noise Study project page.

Integrations & Utility Pipelines

  • Editable Description Pipeline (editable-copy-of-description) – Quickbase/Pipeline utility that lets users work with editable copies of descriptions while keeping system-of-record fields clean.
  • Core Sync & Glue Services – Additional sync jobs (for example, verizon_ariba_core_sync) and pipelines that keep Quickbase, Ariba, and other systems aligned so the automations above always see current data.

Quickbase Architecture & Internal Tools

Many of these automations depend on carefully designed Quickbase apps.

  • Quickbase Integrations & Internal Tools – Tables, relationships, forms, formulas, and reports that support:
    • Pay-period and HR logic for timekeeping
    • Project / site / client structures for telecom deliverables
    • Fields mapped into Excel/Word templates and finance bulk uploads
  • Inventory & Office Liquidation Tools – Tools and spreadsheets to track office assets (chairs, desks, monitors, cabinets, etc.), export structured inventory lists, and support resale or liquidation processes.
